Our Story

Local Teas started as a passion project of its owner, Mrs Idowu Baku. Ever since she was a child growing up in the plains of Plateau state, she has loved tea; its taste, its smell, its calming effect and health benefits.

At her home in Kano, she started planting tea ingredients, so she could drink them fresh, just like she used to back home...
